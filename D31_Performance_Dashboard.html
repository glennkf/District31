Getting Started

  • 1
    Upload your CSV Drag and drop your Division_Performance export from Toastmasters International onto the upload bar. You can drop multiple files at once to build trend history.
  • 2
    Explore the four views District Summary shows district-wide KPIs. Division and Area views let you drill down by leadership level. Trend & History charts progress over time.
  • 3
    Refresh anytime Drop a new CSV whenever TI publishes updated data. Your snapshot history and latest data are saved automatically — no data is lost on refresh.
  • 4
    Tag your snapshots (optional) Use the date picker next to the upload bar to manually label a snapshot if the file's date needs overriding.

Dashboard Guide

  • 1
    Upload CSVDrag one or multiple Division_Performance CSVs onto the upload bar. The most recent file becomes the live view; all files are saved as trend snapshots.
  • 2
    District SummaryTop-level KPIs, division rankings, goals distribution, top 10 clubs, and suspended clubs.
  • 3
    Division ViewSelect a division to see area summaries and a full club breakdown with goal status.
  • 4
    Area ViewSelect a division and area for club-level coaching priorities and goals-to-Distinguished tracking.
  • 5
    Trend & HistoryCharts show Distinguished clubs, at-risk clubs, April renewals %, and total members over time across all saved snapshots.
  • 6
    Duplicate protectionUploading the same data under a different filename is detected automatically — no duplicate snapshots will be created.
