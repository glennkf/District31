Getting Started

  • 1
    Data loads automatically The dashboard fetches the latest club performance data from GitHub on every page load — no upload needed. Just open and explore.
  • 2
    Explore the four views District Summary shows district-wide KPIs — click any stat card to see the clubs behind the number. Division and Area views let you drill down by leadership level. Trend & History charts progress over time.
  • 3
    Distinguished status tiers Clubs are ranked from At-Risk (0–1 goals) through Distinguished, Select, President's, up to Smedley Distinguished (10 goals + 25+ members).
  • 4
    Manual override (Glenn only) Use the upload bar to drag in a new CSV if you need to override GitHub data. A date picker lets you manually label the snapshot.

Dashboard Guide

  • 1
    Auto-loaded dataData is fetched automatically from GitHub on every page load. No upload needed for leaders — just open the dashboard and it's ready.
  • 2
    District SummaryTop-level KPIs with clickable stat cards — click any card (Active Clubs, Distinguished, At-Risk, April Renewed, etc.) to see the exact clubs behind that number.
  • 3
    Division ViewSelect a division to see area summaries and a full club breakdown with goal status.
  • 4
    Area ViewSelect a division and area for club-level coaching priorities and goals-to-Distinguished tracking.
  • 5
    Trend & HistoryCharts show Distinguished clubs, at-risk clubs, April renewals %, and total members over time across all saved snapshots.
  • 6
    Distinguished tiersClubs are classified from At-Risk (0–1 goals) through Distinguished (5+), Select (7+), President's (9+), up to Smedley Distinguished (10 goals + 25+ members).
  • 7
    Manual override (Glenn only)Drag a new CSV onto the upload bar to push fresh data. Duplicate uploads are detected automatically — no duplicate snapshots will be created.
